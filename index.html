About Pratham

With over 8 years of experience in visual storytelling, I've dedicated my career to capturing the essence of life's most precious moments through the lens of artistry and emotion.

My journey began with a simple belief: every story deserves to be told beautifully. Whether it’s the intimate glance between newlyweds, the energy of a corporate event, or the authentic moments that define a brand, I approach each project with the same passion and attention to detail.

Based in New York but available worldwide, I specialize in luxury wedding photography and commercial videography. My style blends cinematic techniques with authentic storytelling, creating visual narratives that resonate long after the moment has passed.
